# Era Doctrines

Doctrines are historical policy modifiers tied to the 1917-1991 era sequence.

| Doctrine | Used By | Gameplay Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `revolutionary` | Revolution | Requisitioning, legitimacy pressure |
| `industrialization` | Collectivization / Industrialization | Higher extraction, production mandates |
| `wartime` | Great Patriotic War | Conscription and war production |
| `reconstruction` | Reconstruction | Rebuilding and recovery pressure |
| `thaw` | Thaw portions | Morale relief, lighter extraction |
| `freeze` | Freeze portions | Crackdown, renewed extraction |
| `stagnation` | Stagnation / dissolution | Paperwork, corruption, productivity decay |

No doctrine exists for future or post-scarcity eras in 1.0.
