# Historical Eras

The playable historical campaign ends in 1991. `stagnation` is the final era ID and is tuned as stagnation plus dissolution.

| ID | Years | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `revolution` | 1917-1921 | Founding scarcity and legitimacy crisis |
| `collectivization` | 1922-1931 | Grain extraction and rural coercion |
| `industrialization` | 1932-1940 | Factories, terror, high quotas |
| `great_patriotic` | 1941-1944 | War economy and conscription |
| `reconstruction` | 1945-1955 | Recovery and heavy rebuilding |
| `thaw_and_freeze` | 1956-1981 | Reform/crackdown oscillation |
| `stagnation` | 1982-1991 | Decay, corruption, reform failure, campaign completion |

After 1991, the era does not advance. Grounded free play continues under the final historical mechanics.
