# Scoring

The historical campaign score is finalized at the 1991 dissolution endpoint.

Score should reflect:

- survival through historical pressure;
- quota performance and report honesty;
- population health and demographic continuity;
- infrastructure condition;
- political standing, arrests, commendations, and failures;
- crisis handling.

Continuing after 1991 is free play with no new campaign-era score escalation. The settlement remains playable for local management and self-directed survival.
