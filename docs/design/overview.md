# Design Overview

SimSoviet 1917 1.0 is one historical campaign, 1917-1991, with grounded post-campaign continuation.

## Pillars

- **Bureaucratic survival**: The state, quotas, inspections, and reports are the primary threat.
- **Organic settlement**: Agents grow and operate the settlement; the player guides instead of hand-placing a city.
- **Historical pressure**: Eras, crises, shortages, war, decay, and political scrutiny create structure.
- **Local consequences**: Post-1991 play remains about the same settlement and its unresolved problems.

## Systems

- Historical era progression.
- Classical pressure and crisis emergence.
- Economy, food, vodka, storage, power, transport, construction, demographics, workers, KGB, loyalty, narrative.
- Building panels and Government HQ tabs as the main interface.

## 1.0 Boundaries

No future eras, space layers, multi-settlement runtime, or post-scarcity mechanics are included in the 1.0 design.
