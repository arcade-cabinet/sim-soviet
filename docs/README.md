## Scope Guardrail

1.0 keeps:

- one settlement;
- historical eras through 1991;
- food, industry, power, transport, politics, KGB, demographics, narrative;
- classical pressure domains and historical crises;
- grounded post-1991 continuation.

1.0 excludes:

- deep-future or Kardashev progression;
- space or celestial gameplay layers;
- multi-settlement relocation or world expansion;
- post-scarcity pressure domains;
- alternate-history product modes.
