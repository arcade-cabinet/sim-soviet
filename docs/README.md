## Kept Scope

- Historical settlement survival.
- Organic local growth.
- Food, industry, power, transport, politics, KGB, demographics, narrative.
- Classical pressure domains and historical crises.
- Grounded local continuation after 1991.

## Removed From 1.0

- Deep future or Kardashev progression.
- Space, world, or per-world timelines.
- Multi-settlement relocation.
- Post-scarcity pressure domains.
- Celestial or megastructure rendering.
