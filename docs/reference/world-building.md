# World-Building Content

Worldbuilding supports the historical 1917-1991 campaign and grounded local continuation.

## Content Types

- Historical campaign timeline events.
- Building flavor text.
- Radio announcements.
- Loading quotes.
- Achievements.
- Settlement and leader names.

## Timeline

`src/content/worldbuilding/timeline.ts` exports `HISTORICAL_TIMELINE` and `getTimelineEvent()`. It covers Soviet historical beats from 1922 through 1991 in official/satirical voice.

## Achievements

Achievements should reward survival, bureaucratic compromises, infrastructure outcomes, historical milestones, and post-1991 local continuation. They should not promise deep-future progression.
